Model Components

Garage Model

The garage model is created to demonstrate the critical elements of our system.

Side Panels

The four walls of the garage model were decorated with a beadboard cut in 1 ½ inches increments and hot glued to the structure to create a ‘ripple’ effect to mimic the siding shingles of a real-life garage structure.

Roof Panels

For the roof of the structure, strip shingles were cut in 6” x 4” and hot glued to the top of the roof.

Stepper motors

The model incorporates two stepper motors and two stepper motor driver boards hinged between the sides of a wooden roller; this will allow the motors to recede and advance the garage door on the ramp by rotating in a clockwise or a counter-clockwise motion when a valid fingerprint is encountered.

model

Structure

The main purpose of the garage model for demonstration purposes. The structure of the prototype was created using a smooth particle board. The panel of the garage door was created from sheet metal cut in 2" and attached using hinges. Wooden dowels are added to the panel to assist the panel from receding and advancing on the ramp smoothly. Also, a wooden roller hinged in between two stepper motors which turn clockwise and counter-clockwise upon recieving an authorized fingerprint. The panel is outwardly lifted using 20" of fishing line, and will perform open and close operation on the panel of the garage based on the encounterment of authorized fingerprint.

Circuit Compartment

A compartment has been created above the 'ceiling' of the strucutre for the circuit components to reside in. The main purpose of this addition is to compartmentalize the circuit mechanisms to reside in. The added board had to be built with a little opening above the floor structure of the gargae to allow flexible space for the panel to move up and down. This opening also allows the stepper motor boards wires to communicate with stepper motors effectively. The ceiling of the structure houses the circuit components of the system including the breadboard, UNO R3(s), manual buttons and two stepper motors driver boards.

LCD screen + FPS Compartment

In order to enclose the LCD screen and the Fingerprint Scanner we have created another compartment. The main purpose of this compartment is to be placed in the front of the garage and act as the user interface for conveniently interacting with the system. Also, this compartment stimulates the real setting of a garage, where users enter their credientials at a keypad in front of the garage.
